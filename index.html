About LBX

In the crypto world, holding assets shouldn't come at the cost of liquidity, akin to 'imprisonment'. LBX innovatively decouples native assets from yield rights through advanced DeFi logic. Users retain underlying asset exposure while gaining highly liquid LBX certificates. This is not just asset digitization, but a secondary release of wealth – allowing every token to freely move across lending, liquidity mining, and cross-chain protocols, achieving multiple layers of yield.
